- Age of 0~9

Honestly, I don't remember clearly what I did at this age, but I'll try to keep my memory alive as much as possible.

First of all, my hometown is Sokcho. Of course, Sokcho is famous for the East Sea. I remember going around with my family a lot when I was young.

And, I traveled to Cambodia with my mom, cousin sisters, aunt, and Maternal grandmother.

Unfortunately, It was before I went to the kindergarten, so I can't remember what I did and what I saw at that time.

But I think it was a very good trip for me, because the memory of this trip remains a happy memory.


I entered Soya Elementary School when I was 8 years old.

But, I went to this school only for the first semester and my family moved to Incheon in the second semester.

The school I went was Gojan Elementary School.





- Age of 10~19

The year of 6th grade's winter vacation, I went abroad to Spain and France with my aunts, uncles, and cousin sisters.

First, I went to Spain, and I went to Sagrada Familia and also Camp Nou to watch soccer.

It was a match between FC Barcelona and AT Madrid.

At that time, there were so many famous players such as Messi, Neymar, Suarez, Griezmann, and Torres!!

It was a really happy moment. The game was a 3-1 victory for Barcelona.

Watching this game may be the top three happiest moments of my life.

After graduating elementary school in Incheon, I moved back to Sokcho.


At the age of 14, I entered Sokcho Middle School.

Until then, my dream was to be a soldier. That was because the drama 'Descendants of the Sun' was popular at that time.

I was attracted to the drama and dreamed of becoming a soldier and prepared to enter the Korea military academy.

I thought I should work harder, so I even got second place in my school.

However, that dream did not last long, and I finally decided to go to a regular high school.


I entered Sokcho High School at the age of 17.

Everyone knows that high school was the most important time.but,,, I was stupid.

I worked hard until the first grade, but I gradually started to play games and neglected my studies.

Now that I think about it, I really regret it. Why did I do that? ...

So I failed my initial goal of In Seoul and it became the most regrettable moment in my life.





- Age of 20~now

At the age of 20, I entered Kangwon University. And my department is computer engineering.

I lived in a university dormitory, and this was my first time living alone in another region.

At first, I was very lonely, but I became close to my roommate and got better soon.


21 years old, on May 9, 2022, I joined the army for 18 months.

After a month of training at the Nonsan Training Center, I was placed in a place called the Communications Company of the 52nd Brigade of the 12th Division in Wontong, Inje.

There, I was a communications soldier (system installation soldier).

I didn't want to spend any meaningless time in the military.So I learned my specialty and exercised harder than anyone else.

As a result, I served as a squad commander and got a special fighter and advanced to the sergeant early.

Surely it was a really meaningful moment.


In June 2023, I had a really hard training called KCTC.

It was a mock war with other units, and it was the biggest training. I spent two weeks in a tent and in a car.

During this training, I was able to solve the question of what I would do if a person was in an extreme situation. It was a training that I learned a lot.

I went on vacation in August, and went on a trip to Busan with my younger brother.

When I heard Paris-Saint-German FC was invited to Korea, I decided that I had to watch the game between PSG and Jeonbuk in Busan.

I was a big fan of Neymar, and Lee Kang-in had transfer to that team. So, for me, it was lucky.

On November 8, I was finally discharged from the military. It was the happiest moment in my whole life. For sure, can't forget that Happiness until I die.

Three days later, I went to Waterpia for a part-time job. Now that I think about it, I think I lived really hard.


I worked part-time job until January 2024 in Waterpia, and returned to university in March.

Since then, I have been attending college and am satisfied with my own. I will definitely live harder in the future!!
